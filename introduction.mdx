## What is Pranthora ?

Pranthora lets you create voice agents and workflows with built-in integrations to multiple providers.\
Simply plug in what you need, and your agent is ready to handle complex, real-world tasks — from automations to conversations — right out of the box.

What you can do with Pranthora **Voice Agents**

- **Build lifelike voice experiences** that understand and respond naturally to your users.
- **Make and receive calls** seamlessly through telephony or web-based channels.
- **Connect instantly to multiple providers** with Pranthora’s unified MCP integrations — no complex setup required.
- **Automate complex workflows** like appointment scheduling, lead qualification, or customer support.

What you can do with Pranthora **Voice Workflows**

- **Design conversational flows visually** — define how the conversation moves step by step.
- **Assign roles to agents** — let each agent handle a specific part of the dialogue based on its purpose.
- **Set transition rules** — control when the conversation moves to the next step or agent.
- **Connect actions and integrations** — let certain steps perform real tasks like scheduling or fetching data automatically.

## Key Capabilities of Pranthora Voice Agents

Pranthora provides powerful, flexible, and easy-to-use voice agents that enable natural, controlled, and intelligent interactions across multiple channels.

### **Core Features**

- **Easily Configurable Components**  
  Quickly set up Speech-to-Text (STT), Text-to-Speech (TTS), and LLM-based conversational logic without complex coding.  

- **Wide Range of Provider Integrations** *(Heart of the System)*  
  Our extensive integration support allows agents to connect with existing systems, enabling them to perform complex tasks seamlessly.  

- **Multi-Channel Access**  
  Interact with agents via **phone numbers**, **web**, or **WhatsApp**, giving your users flexible ways to communicate.  

- **Natural Conversations with Turn-Taking**  
  Agents understand context, manage turn-taking, and maintain coherent, human-like dialogue.  

- **Controlled Workflows & Transfers**  
  Design workflows to guide conversations, manage branching logic, and transfer interactions to other agents or humans when needed.  

- **Embedded and Web-Ready**  
  Generate embedded code for your website or system, spin up any agent instantly, and leverage the upcoming SDK for deeper integrations.  

### **Why It Matters**

By combining wide-ranging integrations, multi-channel access, and robust workflow control, Pranthora empowers businesses to deliver **consistent, intelligent, and reliable voice interactions**, whether for customer support, appointment scheduling, or any other automated conversational task.
